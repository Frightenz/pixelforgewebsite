Quick help topics.

Include your device model, iOS or iPadOS version, PixelForge version, and a short description of what happened when you email support.

Restore purchases

Open the app's purchase screen and use restore purchases. Confirm you are signed into the Apple Account that made the purchase.

Model downloads

Use Wi-Fi when possible, keep PixelForge open during large downloads, and confirm there is enough local storage available.

Generation issues

Try a shorter prompt, a smaller image size, or a different model. Restart the app if generation appears stuck.

Privacy lock

If privacy lock is enabled, use your device authentication to unlock PixelForge. Permission settings are managed in iOS or iPadOS Settings.
